Latitude vs. Wind Speed

As we can see in the graph, the wind speed seems to slightly increase as the latitude increases. The wind speed seems to be stagnant from -60 latitude to 80 latitude, with occasional spikes on 60 latitude. Locations farther from the equator may result in higher wind speeds and I would have to look into this further to see why there are spikes.
